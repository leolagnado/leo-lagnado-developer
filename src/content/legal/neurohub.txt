Privacy Policy
Welcome to the Privacy Policy for NeuroHub ("we", "us", or "our"), a free mobile
application. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your information while you use
our app. By using NeuroHub, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Please note that this app does not require user registration, and our data collection
practices are minimal. Our goal is to protect your privacy and provide a secure and
enjoyable user experience.

Information We Collect

NeuroHub collects the following data, which is not linked to your identity:

   a. Location: This optional data is collected to center the map in the app based on
      your current location.

   b. Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, including the
      duration and frequency of your sessions. We also transmit the geographical
      coordinates of the map (based on the area being viewed by the user as they
      navigate) to two places:

          o Firebase Firestore Database: the database is queried to find locations that
            were previously rated and return them to the app to be displayed on the
            map (rated locations are never connected to any specific user)

          o OpenAI's ChatGPT API: a request is made to suggest sensory friendly
            places and return them to the app to be displayed on the map

      Please note that these coordinates do not represent the user's actual location, but
      rather the area being viewed on the map as navigated by the user.

   c. Diagnostics: Standard usage statistics and crash data that help maintain the app's
      functionality and improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

   1. To ensure the app's proper functioning and troubleshoot any issues that may
      arise.
   2. To analyze and improve the app's performance, features, and overall user
      experience.
   3. To generate contextually relevant responses within the app

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share
